01 · Overview

About

I'm a software tester currently completing my Work-Integrated Learning placement to finish my Diploma in Computer Systems Engineering. My focus is manual and functional testing, reading requirements, writing clear test cases, executing them across environments, and logging defects with enough detail that a developer can reproduce and fix them on the first pass. I'm comfortable working from a test plan, but just as comfortable exploring a feature on my own to find edge cases a script would miss.
